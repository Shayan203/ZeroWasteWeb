Creative Leftovers

  • Turn leftover rice into fried rice or pudding.
  • Transform stale bread into breadcrumbs or croutons.
  • Use vegetable peels for making broth or chips.

Proper Storage

  • Use clear, labeled containers for easy access.
  • Wrap leafy greens in damp paper towels.
  • Follow the FIFO method: First In, First Out.

Composting Basics

  • Compost fruit peels, coffee grounds, and eggshells.
  • Avoid composting meat, dairy, or oily foods.
  • Use compost as fertilizer for your garden.
